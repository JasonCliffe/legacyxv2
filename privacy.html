PRIVACY

How LegacyXV2 handles enquiries, security checks, and privacy-friendly analytics.

GDPR Security Transparency

Last updated:

Who we are

Controller: LegacyXV2 (website owner / operator).

If you have privacy questions or want to exercise your data rights, contact us via the Merch enquiry form (recommended) or email using the address provided in the footer of messages you receive from us.

Scope: This notice covers this website (legacyxv2.co.uk), including the Merch and Drop One enquiry forms.

What we collect

  • Enquiry form data (optional): name and email (if you provide them).
  • Enquiry message data: topic and message content.
  • Security/anti-spam: Cloudflare Turnstile verification signals (to prevent automated abuse).
  • Basic traffic analytics: aggregated website measurement via Cloudflare Web Analytics (privacy-friendly, cookie-less).

Why we use it

  • To respond to enquiries about merch, sizing, availability, and requests.
  • To protect the site from spam and automated abuse.
  • To understand site performance (high-level measurement) so we can improve content and reliability.

Legal basis (GDPR)

We process enquiry data under legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f)) to communicate with you and run the website securely. Where an enquiry relates to a future purchase, processing may also be necessary to take steps at your request prior to entering into a contract (Article 6(1)(b)).

You can object to processing based on legitimate interests. If you object, we will stop unless we have compelling legitimate grounds to continue or we need it for legal claims.

Who we share data with

  • Cloudflare (hosting, security, and Turnstile anti-spam).
  • Resend (email delivery for enquiry notifications).

These providers process data on our behalf to deliver the service (e.g., transmit your enquiry email, block spam).

International transfers

Our service providers may process data in countries outside the UK/EEA. Where required, appropriate safeguards are used (such as contractual protections) to help protect your information.

Retention

Enquiry data is retained for up to 90 days to allow follow-up and resolution, unless we must keep it longer for legal or compliance reasons.

Your rights

  • Access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure: request deletion (subject to legal requirements).
  • Restriction: ask us to limit processing in certain cases.
  • Objection: object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Complaint: you can complain to the UK ICO (Information Commissioner’s Office) if needed.

To request any of the above, use the Merch enquiry form and write “Privacy request” in the topic.

Cookies

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics for privacy-friendly measurement. It is designed to work without using tracking cookies for behavioural advertising. Security services (like Turnstile) may set technical data to protect the site.
