Q1 2026 Recommendations

Best budget CPUs/GPUs for gaming builds as of Q1 2026 — split into AM4, Intel (DDR4/DDR5), and AM5.

AM4 CPUs Budget → High
  • Low-end: Ryzen 5 5500
  • Mid-tier: Ryzen 7 5700X
  • High-end: Ryzen 7 5800X3D
Intel CPUs DDR4 / DDR5
  • Low-end: Core i5-12400F
  • Mid-tier: Core i7-14700KF
  • High-end: Core i9-14900K / 14900KF (after latest BIOS update)
AM5 CPUs Budget → High
  • Low-end: Ryzen 5 7500F
  • Mid-range: Ryzen 5 7600X3D or Ryzen 7 7700X
  • High-end: Ryzen 7 7800X3D or Ryzen 7 9800X3D
GPU — Low-end Value
  • RTX 5060
  • AMD RX 9060
GPU — Mid-range Best all-round
  • RTX 5070 / 5070 Ti
  • AMD RX 9060 XT / 9070
GPU — High-end Top tier
  • RTX 5080 / 5090
  • AMD RX 9070 XT

Notes:
• Ensure BIOS is updated and all drivers are up to date. This will be updated when new products have been reviewed.
