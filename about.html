The "LegacyXV2" Name

People often ask where the name comes from. The "Legacy" part is a throwback to the 2010 WWE stable featuring Randy Orton, Cody Rhodes, and Ted DiBiase Jr. The "XV2" is a nod to my roots and the group of friends I had in a Black Ops 2 sniping clan. Combining those worlds built the brand you see today.

Console Roots & Hardware Passion

My first console was the Xbox 360—the greatest of all time in my opinion—and it's where I played my favorite game: Modern Warfare 2 (2009). My journey into PC hardware started more recently; I built my first PC in 2023. It was a scary and satisfying experience, but highly rewarding, and it sparked my passion for showcasing how technology evolves.

The Completionist Vibe: I don’t just "beat" games. I 100% them. Whether it’s a deep story or an achievement-heavy title, I showcase everything the game has to offer.

The Ethics of the Channel

I am always open to working with tech and gaming companies that push the industry forward—brands like ASRock, Corsair, Noctua, Capcom, or NordVPN. However, I have a strict rule: I will not work with any company that harms animals or fails to protect wildlife.
