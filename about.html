The Origins: Legacy & XV2

People often ask where the name comes from. "Legacy" is a nod to the 2010 WWE stable featuring Randy Orton, Cody Rhodes, and Ted DiBiase Jr. The "XV2" part of my name came from a group of friends I had in a Black Ops 2 sniping clan. Combining those worlds built the foundation of what I do today.

Xbox Roots & PC Evolution

My first console was the Xbox 360—the greatest of all time in my opinion—and it's where I played my favorite game: Modern Warfare 2 (2009). While my roots are in console gaming, I built my first PC in 2023. It was a scary but highly rewarding experience that sparked my passion for showcasing tech evolution to everyone.

The Completionist Approach: I don’t just "beat" games. I 100% them. Whether it’s a deep story or an achievement-heavy title, I showcase everything the game has to offer.
